We Care About Our Sources

We think it's important to be able to know what you're putting into your body. That's why we pay carefully select what farms we source our produce from. We get our delicious organic food from dozens of farms in the surrounding area, including from Agra, Pauma Valley, Descanso, and Deluz.

The Mediterranean-like climate of these Southern California farms provides great growing conditions and growing seasons to produce a large variety of produce, giving you many options to choose from!
