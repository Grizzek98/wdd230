Who We Are

At Bountiful Foods we believe in fresh produce grown locally for our families and communities. Bountiful Foods was started in 2010 by Tom McKinley and his wife Barbara Jane. Tom and Barb raised their three children in a farm in Southern California. Tom and Barb are passionate about community-building and sustaining the the environment and they made build relationships with the other farmers in the area. To support their friends, they created Bountiful Foods: an easy way for families to find and purchase local organic food.

Our mission is to make farm-fresh food more accessible to all families in the Southern California area. If you are interested in donating to or supporting Bountiful Foods in other ways, give us a call via the contact info below.
