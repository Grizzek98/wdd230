Lidekonia Chamber of Commerce Target Audience

Target Audience

This site is meant to target small business owners and non-profit organizations within the Lidekonia area, as well as any other organizations that will mutually benefit the business ecosystem as a whole.

Small Businesses

This site will help small businesses whether they are just starting up or not. Our goal is to help with networking, exposure and advertising, enabling community involvement, and even providing free resources to help shape small businesses from the ground up. We hope to be able to answer the following questions, among many more:

  • How do I efficiently reach out to my target audience?
  • How do I find the resources I need to start my own business?
  • How do I find local events at which I can network and even market my business?

Meet Joe

Biographical Information

Joe is 53 years old. He owns a beautiful flower shop with his sweet wife, which they built and now manage together for 14 years. They have been doing well in this business.

Goals and Needs

His goal is to hire some additional employees to help out, as he and his wife are getting older and need more support.

How We Can Help

The Chamber can connect him with local accountants to help him set up and prepare for the tax considerations of hiring additional employees. He also gets connected with a health insurance broker who helps him get insurance benefits set up for his employees. His friends at the Chamber also help him build his help wanted advertisements.

Non-Profit Organizations

We also hope to enable the growth of non-profit organizations in our area. The same resources we we listed above are available to non-profits. They will have immediate access to our network of businesses to be able to find their target audience. We hope to be able to enable non-profits to have all the resources they need to adequately organize local events and projects, which will in turn serve to strengthen our community as a whole. We hope to answer these questions and more:

  • How can I get better exposure to raise more money for my non-profit?
  • How can I use local businesses to quickly and effectively get the word out for my events?
  • How can I find the right people to network with for my non-profit?

Meet Michelle

Biographical Information

Michelle is starting a non-profit organization to support mothers with autistic children. She is a 42 year old mother of five, herself. She and her husband own a small business, which helps supply restaurants cash registers and other payment method machines and software.

Goals and Needs

Michelle wants to start this non-profit organization because she has an autistic child and understands that mothers with autistic children need respite, resources and support. She is looking to organize a five-hour retreat on a Saturday for these mothers that will be completely free for them.

How We Can Help

The Chamber can help Michelle connect to business owners who are looking for tax deductions for charitable contributions and special advertising opportunities. Small businesses such as restaurants could provide food at the retreat, salons could provide pampering and local botiques could provide small gifts and favors.
