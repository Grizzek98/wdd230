Labore Quis Fundraiser

Labore Quis, a non-profit organization dedicated to completely eradicating all human sickness, is holding a fundraiser July 8th at Dolor Park. Apparently eradicating all disease is a very simple task, they just need enough money to get started. Come to Dolor Park on July 8th to donate money while holding puppies and kittens, eating delicious pie, and playing a variety of lawn games. There will also be an auction where you will be able to bid on those wonderful puppies and kittens to be able to take them home.
