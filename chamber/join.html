Membership Benefits

Non-Profit Membership

No membership fee. Includes free advertising on Home Page Spotlight, and discounted access to training courses at $30* each.

Bronze Membership

$25 monthly membership fee. Home Page Spotlight advertising offered at $30 for two weeks, training courses offered $90* each.

Silver Membership

$50 monthly membership fee. Includes one free two-week Home Page Spotlight advertisement (terms apply**), then offered at $10 for two weeks, training courses offered at $75* each.

Gold Membership

$75 monthly membership fee. Includes free advertising on Home Page Spotlight, and discounted access to training courses at $30* each.

*Training courses are a one-time payment, but several respective courses are offered, each costing the same amount.
